# Banner Design

## Supported Platforms
Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram, Google Display, website heroes, print formats

## Art Direction Styles (22)
Minimalist, gradient, bold typography, photo-based, illustrated, geometric, retro, glassmorphism, 3D, neon, duotone, editorial, collage, and more

## Design Constraints
- Content within central 70-80% of canvas (safe zones)
- Maximum 2 typefaces per banner
- Single CTA button minimum 44px height
- Text occupies under 20% for ad compliance

## Workflow
1. Gather requirements (purpose, platform, content, brand, style, quantity)
2. Research & art direction
3. Build HTML/CSS banners + generate AI visuals
4. Export to PNG at exact platform dimensions
5. Iterate based on feedback
